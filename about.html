About

Bradley Korabik is the man behind the lens at Boat Daddy Marine. He graduated from the University of North Carolina Wilmington in 2017 with a degree in Computer Science and Business Minor. During his time in Wilmington Bradley enjoyed fishing and working on a vareity of different boats. His love of boating started at a young age going out on Lake Michigan with his Grandpa. To keep in touch with his passion for boating Bradley started Boat Daddy Marine, LLC, which originated in 2018 as a marine parts company that has transitioned into a photography company. Boat Daddy Marine has an official Instagram account, (@boatdaddymarine) where boat photos are posted daily and has amassed over 8,000 followers and counting. Bradley takes his camera with him as he travels around the Wilmington, North Carolina area capturing different perspectives from on the water and around different marinas.


Boat Daddy Marine works with boat brokers, dealers, and owners who are looking for professional photography of their boat.
